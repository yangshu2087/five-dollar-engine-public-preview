The problem

No task contract

Work starts before the goal, constraints, non-goals, and verification are clear.

Weak evidence

Outputs are accepted because they look plausible, not because they were verified.

Unsafe automation

External actions, spending, account access, and publishing need clear human approval gates.

No compounding

Lessons stay trapped in old chats instead of becoming reusable templates and checklists.

What is included

Daily operating checklist

A start/end-of-day workflow for making AI work explicit and reusable.

Task contract template

Goal, context, constraints, non-goals, done criteria, verification, and approval boundaries.

Verification checklist

A ladder of proof before work is called complete.

Automation risk boundaries

Green/yellow/red zones for deciding what AI may do, propose, or never do automatically.

Retrospective template

A short loop for turning mistakes and friction into updated workflows.

ROI tracker

Measure time saved, rework, cash cost, revenue, and quality.
