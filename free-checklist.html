10 Checks Before You Let AI Automate a Workflow

  1. What exact outcome should change?
  2. What data or account access is involved?
  3. What action would be irreversible or costly?
  4. Where is the human approval gate?
  5. What is the smallest safe test?
  6. What evidence proves success?
  7. What log will preserve decisions and outputs?
  8. What failure would trigger rollback?
  9. What should never be automated?
  10. What will be reused or improved next time?
