AI automation ROI calculator: decide what to automate first

Use this manual worksheet before buying tools, granting account access, or asking AI to touch a recurring workflow. The goal is not a perfect forecast; it is a clear enough estimate to choose the next safe test.

The 10-minute worksheet

Input Manual estimate How to estimate conservatively
Hours saved per week ___ Count only repeated work that already happens every week.
Hourly value ___ Use the lower of your billable rate or replacement value.
Monthly tool cost ___ Include subscriptions, usage fees, hosting, and review tooling.
Review/rework cost ___ Add time for human review, failed runs, and rollback.
Risk reserve ___ Subtract extra margin for sensitive data, public output, or irreversible actions.

Formula: monthly ROI = weekly hours saved × hourly value × 4 - monthly tool cost - review/rework cost - risk reserve.

Pick the first automation candidate

Green-light candidate

High repeat frequency, low account risk, clear before/after evidence, and a human approval gate before anything public or paid happens.

Yellow-light candidate

Potential value is real, but evidence is weak. Run a local-only dry run and capture screenshots, logs, or reviewed samples first.

Red-light candidate

Requires payment settings, private customer data, legal/financial claims, or public posting before a safe local test exists.

Boundary: No checkout, email capture, ad spend, or income result is promised.
